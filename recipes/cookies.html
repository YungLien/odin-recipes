Toasty Campfire Cookies

Description

I created these cookies while camping, they are the tastiest campfire cookies you will ever eat! You can also make them at home over the stove.

Ingredients

  • 5 (1 ounce) squares chopped white chocolate
  • 20 oatmeal cookies
  • 1 (1.55 ounce) bar milk chocolate candy
  • 1 cup miniature marshmallows

Steps

  1. Melt white chocolate in a double boiler, spread evenly over the bottoms of the cookies.
  2. Break up the milk chocolate bar into 10 pieces, and place 1 piece onto the white chocolate side of 10 cookies.
  3. Place the marshmallows on top of the milk chocolate.
  4. Top with the remaining cookies, creating a chocolate-marshmallow sandwich.
  5. Toast cookies over a campfire, or stove using tongs until the chocolate and marshmallow has melted, about 3 minutes.
